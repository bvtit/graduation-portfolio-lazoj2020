Sophomore PC Build

  1. Home
  2. Portfolio Item

Every year the IT sophomores have the opportunity to design and build a PC completely customized by the class. We use a website called pcpartpicker.com which allows us to design and price out many different PC builds. After everyone creates a build design we present and vote on our favorite design. Then the parts are ordered and everyone gets to build their own. We then use these PCs for the remainder of our time in the shop.
