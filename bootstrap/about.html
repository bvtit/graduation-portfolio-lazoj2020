About Me

My name is Jacob Lazo, I am currently a 11th grade student at BVT in the Information Technology and Networking shop. Over the past years in shop I have completed many assignments and projects some of which are displayed throughout the website.

During my first year and a half in shop we focused on the CompTIA A+ curriculum which primarilly focused on computer hardware and software repair skills giving us an in-depth look at the interworkings of a computer and how to troubleshoot systems in the event of a failure.

Throughout the second half of my sophomore year and the entirety of my junior year, the focus was mainly on the Cisco I and II curriculums. Cisco I focused on the basic configuration of a network including IP addresses and basic switch and router commands to allow a network to function. Cisco II went more in depth into the topics learned in Cisco I and build upon them to allow us to create networks with more advanced security and functionality.
