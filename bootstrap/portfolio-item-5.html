Project Description

At the end of out Cisco I curriculum, we were assigned a project to demonstrate out knowledge of the topics learned throughout the course. We were instructed to create a network topology consisting of 3 routers, 4 switches and 4 pcs. We were assigned the base subnet 172.16.1.0 and instructed to properly assign IP addressess to the devices within the network. We created a document simulating how we would go about proposing the new network design to an actual company in the field including invoices and proof of functionality tests.
