Customer iPad Repair

Every now and again customers will bring in their devices for us to fix them. This time we had an iPad that had a broken screen and power problem. Whenever the device would be plugged in and powered on, it would display the Apple logo then restart and enter a boot loop. We narrowed the problem down to a power delivery issue and ordered the parts to fix the problem promptly.
