Project Description

We had a customer bring in their iPhone which had been damaged and asked us to fix it. We narrowed the problem down to a problem with the device's OLED display panel which was causing a white bar to appear on the screen, effectively rendering the device unuseable. We ordered a new screen and I worked to replace and test the new screen before returning it to the client.
